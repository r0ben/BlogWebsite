Title: Lalu Yadav, Rabri Devi & their daughter Misa get bail in land for jobs scam cas

Content: Former Bihar CMs Lalu Yadav and Rabri Devi were on Wednesday granted bail by a Delhi court in connection with the land-for-jobs scam. Lalu and Rabri's daughter Misa Bharti was also granted bail in the case. Lalu Yadav, who recently underwent kidney transplant, was seen entering the Rouse Avenue Court in a wheelchair on Wednesday.
